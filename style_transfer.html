How I Turned My Cat into a Painting

Using ConvNets to transfer the artistic style of one image to another

Style transfer can automatically transform photos

Neural Artistic Style Transfer

Using Neural Artistic Style Transfer (Gatys, et al. 2015) I was able to transfer the artistic style of different painting onto my cat.

Neural Artistic Style Transfer uses a VGG-19 pretrained on ImageNet. The style of an image is captured by the activations it generates in the VGG-19. Then the content image is iteratively changed to match its style to the style image.

I wrote an article on how style transfer works and my results on Medium.

Check out the full code on Github, or the interactive notebook on Google Colab.
